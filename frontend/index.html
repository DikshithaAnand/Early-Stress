About MindScan

MindScan is an AI-powered mental stress detection system that analyzes simple daily lifestyle inputs to estimate your current stress level and provide actionable recommendations.

The system uses a Random Forest Classifier trained on lifestyle-stress correlation data. The model analyzes five key factors — sleep quality, workload, social media consumption, screen time, and self-reported mood — to classify stress into three levels.

This tool is designed for early awareness, not clinical diagnosis. If you're experiencing persistent or severe stress, please reach out to a mental health professional.
